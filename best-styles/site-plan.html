Purpose and FAQ

Home page

Purpose Beventful is site that will turn your dreams into reality. We provide services that cater for an event that you would like to host. We vary from birthday parties, wedding ceremonies, church events and so forth. I believe that people will come to my site because firstly we specialise in event planning in which many people love to go all out in terms of events. When people see what good service that we do then they will be more thrilled to let others know what we do. I also know that when something is new in the market people usually want to check it out so since I am starting this new site, they will be thrilled to see what is unique about us and what our price range is. FAQ When people will come to the site, I believe that the first question they will ask is what are your price ranges? We will have a price range in which the starting price is R350 which will expand as to R3000 depending on what the client wants. The list includes what the different price ranges incorporate for each event being planned. Another question that we will be asked is How many days do you guys’ work? We will be working from Monday to Friday a full 8 hours but exceptions can be made for Saturday for working half a day. We will have a landline number that you will be able to call. Will your company be accessible for bookings online? Yes, there will be in which it will make it easier for one book from the luxury of their home. Our services We at Beventful offer various activities. We are able to create a birthday parties, baby showers, work parties, graduation evenings, wedding proposals. We believe that these activities will evoke an emotion of how affordable these activities are for a special occasion. We aim to help the visitors see how all they want is a button away. We will have an office in which visitors will be given a chance to walk into what they desire. As a site will have mini stations in which it will cover all that we do as a company. It will be a test run before the actual event. The visitor will just need to pick which one they want and the location in which it will happen.

How to sign up and Uniqueness

How to sign up. What we will require as a site is when the visitor has made an appointment online, they will have to come in person to sign a contract that will state that we have an agreement between the two parties. That agreement will include all the terms and conditions of what we do as a site. After this a deposit will be paid to our site in which the full amount will be paid on the day before the event. The client will have to give us the instructions as to what we have to assist in certain things going the way that they would like us design. What makes UNIQUE What I aim to get out of this site is to break the norm within the society. What I believe is that an event site should not only be focused on one specific thing but rather a collective on what the visitor would like to aim to achieve. That is one thing that may attract more people my site because we offer a variety of items. That is one thing that I know that I will be able to do is help the visitors see what we are capable of achieving and how we can work together to stay.
